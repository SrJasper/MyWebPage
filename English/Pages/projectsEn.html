Stock Tracker
In this project, I chose to apply the most modern technologies on the market, using TypeScript throughout its development. On the server-side, I opted to use the NestJS framework, known for its scalability and modularized structure. For communication with the database, I chose Prisma, which offers a secure and practical interface. PostgreSQL was selected for its reliability and performance. As for hosting, Neon service was chosen for the PostgreSQL database. For the front-end, I used the React library to provide versatility and convenience in structuring the pages.
